# **App Name**: CasaNala.com.mx

## Core Features:

- Interactive Menu: Display an interactive menu with detailed options, prices and real-time updates.
- Online Ordering (Pickup): Enable basic online ordering for pickup at the restaurant.
- Online Ordering (Delivery): Enable basic online ordering for delivery and prepare for Didi and uber eats connections / integrations.
- Geofencing Map: Show the restaurant's location and a geofence area of 10 km around Clinica 14 del IMSS on a Google Map.
- AI Chatbot: Provide a chatbot to answer calls and whatsapp orders, customer questions and make personalized recommendations. The LLM used will be a tool to decide when to incorporate data about the location of the user.
- Admin Panel: Display a panel for admins to update dishes, promotions, and hours. Human resources, integrate recommended basic ERP features for restaurant.
- Screen Touch Capabilities: Include screen touch capabilities for accessibility as recommended for kitchen , orders and ERP integration.
- Logo: Include CASA NALA logo in the app
- Deploy: Deploy the app

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Wine (#6C3A3A) to reflect the brand's identity.
- Background color: Warm Cream (#F5E8D2) for a welcoming feel.
- Accent color: Earthy Mustard to highlight calls to action.
- Artisanal typography (Poiret One-like) to convey the essence of Mexican food.
- Responsive and minimalist design with an artisanal and contemporary style.
